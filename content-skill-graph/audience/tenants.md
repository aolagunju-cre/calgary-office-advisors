# Audience: Calgary Tenants — Calgary Office Advisors

## Who They Are

Business owners, operations leaders, CFOs, founders planning to move, expand, or sign their first Calgary office lease. Making significant real estate decisions with real budget impact. Not CRE experts themselves — they're running their business and need reliable guidance.

## What They Want

- Accurate Calgary CRE market information (vacancy, rents, submarkets)
- Specific advice on what to negotiate and how
- Help understanding the lease terms they don't know
- Confidence that they're not getting a bad deal
- One trusted source who represents their interests

## How to Talk to Them

- Educational, not condescending. they know their business, they need help with real estate
- Specific and actionable: "here's how to calculate whether the TI allowance is fair" not "negotiate carefully"
- Data-backed where possible, clear caveats when estimating
- Reassurance that makes sense: "most tenants make this mistake" + "here's how to avoid it"
- Not anti-landlord, but clearly tenant-aligned

## Their Questions

- "How much does office space cost in Calgary?"
- "Which Calgary neighborhood is right for my business?"
- "What should I negotiate in my lease?"
- "Am I getting a fair deal?"
- "What are the pitfalls to avoid?"
- "How do I know if I should renew or move?"

## Keywords That Resonate

Calgary office space, lease negotiation, TI allowance, vacancy rate, Class A/B, NNN, sublease, right of first refusal, tenant representation
