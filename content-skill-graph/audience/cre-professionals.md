# Audience: CRE Professionals — Calgary Office Advisors

## Who They Are

Other CRE brokers, tenant reps, landlords, and advisors in the Calgary market. Following content to stay informed, find referral opportunities, or check competitive positioning.

## What They Want

- Accurate Calgary CRE market data they can use or reference
- Market intelligence and trend analysis
- Perspectives from the tenant side of the table
- Potential referral relationships

## How to Talk to Them

- Professional, data-driven, collegial tone
- Share market observations they find valuable
- Open to respectful debate on market conditions
- Don't be afraid to have a clear take

## What They Don't Want

- Content that sounds like it's trying to steal their clients
- Inaccurate market data
- Content that trivializes the complexity of CRE transactions
