# Hook Formulas — Calgary Office Advisors

Use these to open every post. Match hook type to platform and topic.

## The Market Data Hook
"[Specific metric] in [Calgary submarket] right now:"
- Best on: LinkedIn, blog
- Examples:
  - "Downtown Calgary vacancy hit [X]% in Q1 2026. Here's what that means for you."
  - "[X] Class A buildings are now offering [Y] months free. Here's how to negotiate it."

## The Tenant Mistake Hook
"Most Calgary businesses get [X] wrong when [specific situation]. Here's what to do instead."
- Best on: LinkedIn, blog
- Examples:
  - "Most tenants get the TI allowance negotiation wrong. Here's the play."
  - "The #1 mistake Calgary businesses make when renewing their lease."

## The Neighborhood Hook
"[Calgary neighborhood] vs [another]: which is actually better for [specific use case]"
- Best on: blog, LinkedIn
- Examples:
  - "East Village vs Beltline: which is better for a 20-person team?"
  - "Why industries are clustering in specific Calgary submarkets"

## The Negotiation Hook
"Negotiate [this specific term], not [the vague thing everyone asks about]"
- Best on: LinkedIn, blog
- Examples:
  - "Negotiate the TI allowance, not the rent. Here's why."
  - "Stop asking for 'free rent' and start asking for the right TI package."

## The Case Study Hook
"[Company type] just signed a [X]-year lease in [Calgary area]. Here's what they learned."
- Best on: LinkedIn, blog
- Examples:
  - "A 50-person tech firm just moved to East Village. Here's what they negotiated."
  - "We helped a founder avoid this common lease mistake. Here's the story."

## Rules
- Data must be accurate and sourced. don't make up vacancy rates or rental figures
- Update this file as market conditions change — numbers go stale
- Track which hook types perform best per platform
