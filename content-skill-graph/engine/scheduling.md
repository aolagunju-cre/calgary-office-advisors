# Scheduling — Calgary Office Advisors

## Weekly Frequency

| Platform | Posts/Week | Best Days |
|---|---|---|
| Blog | 2 | Tue, Thu |
| LinkedIn | 3-4 | Mon, Wed, Thu, Fri |
| Newsletter | 1 | Wed morning |

## Peak Posting Times (Mountain Time)

| Platform | Best Times |
|---|---|---|
| Blog | Publish Tue/Thu (SEO consistency matters) |
| LinkedIn | 7-8am, 12pm, 5-6pm |
| Newsletter | 8-10am Wednesday |

## Batch Workflow

Weekly (Sunday or Monday):
1. Choose 1-2 CRE topics for the week
2. Run the [[repurpose]] chain for each topic
3. This gives you 2 blog posts + 2-4 LinkedIn posts + 1 newsletter for the week
4. Schedule everything

Daily (10 min):
1. Check engagement on morning LinkedIn posts
2. Reply to comments
3. Monitor blog traffic if analytics available

## Content Pillars (rotate through)

1. **Market updates** — vacancy rates, rent trends, submarket comparisons
2. **Negotiation tactics** — specific terms to negotiate, common mistakes to avoid
3. **Neighborhood profiles** — Calgary areas, pros/cons for different business types
4. **Tenant guides** — how to lease office space in Calgary, step by step
5. **Case studies** — anonymized real deals, what was negotiated, lessons learned
6. **Market timing** — when to sign, when to wait, signals to watch

## What Not to Post

- Content that reads like landlord marketing
- Vacancy rate figures without sourcing (or clear caveat if estimated)
- "Calgary is amazing" without substance
- Generic CRE content that applies to any city
- Anything that could be construed as investment advice
